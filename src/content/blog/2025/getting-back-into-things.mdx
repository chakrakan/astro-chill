## Welcoming myself back

It's been a while!

Personally, there was a lot going on since I last posted, and it was time I really had to take a step back and prioritize a lot of different things that I never really had to prioritize previously in my life. 

> So I did exactly that.

I've had lots of growth, development, learnings, changes in perspective, and more generally, new insights that I believe one only gets to truly appreciate once they take a step back to see the bigger picture. 


With that said, I would like to welcome myself back to this blog which kind of got left in the dust. 
We can get right back into things where we left off, and boy do I have updates.

There are no grandeur promises of sticking to any particular cadences, but I would like to be more consistent here. So do expect more discussions on the various topics that are coming up in my day-to-day life.
